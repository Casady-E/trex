Lithium-ion Battery Recycling

We partner with recyclers around the world to provide efficient, affordable, and sustainable solutions to recycling Lithium-ion batteries.

Safely and sustainably extracting resources from every Lithium battery

End-of-life batteries are full of valuable materials that do not degrade when they are recycled. Our recovery and recycle process helps conserve natural resources while ensuring that every battery is handled safely. By partnering with key recyclers, we are able to offer complete end-of-life services including Lithium-ion, alkaline, carbon zinc, lead gel acid, and lithium batteries.

The Power of Collaboration: Realizing a Circular Economy

We partner with leading organizations to provide comprehensive lithium-ion battery recycling solutions. Our partners offer innovative technologies for creating a sustainable, closed-loop supply chain for battery-grade materials, offering an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional mining and high-temperature recycling. They focus on maximizing the value of end-of-life EV batteries through safe handling and efficient recycling, keeping these valuable materials out of landfills and powering future EVs. We collaborate with experts specializing in the safe recovery, transportation, and processing of all battery types, offering services ranging from manufacturer recalls to end-of-life management and utilizing specialized training and technology to mitigate risks.

Our partners recover valuable materials like nickel, cobalt, and lithium from end-of-life lithium-ion batteries, creating a closed-loop system and reducing reliance on unsustainable mining practices, while prioritizing safety and cost-effectiveness in their operations. We also recognize the importance of resource conservation and partner with companies that view waste as a resource, recovering valuable battery metals to reduce the need for virgin mining and minimize environmental impact. Our partners utilize advanced recycling processes, including hydrometallurgical methods, to maximize material recovery and ensure the quality of recovered materials for reuse in battery manufacturing.

Our Process

1. Collection

We collaborate with you to determine the optimal collection method for your lithium-ion batteries and arrange safe, international shipping to our partner facilities. Our global logistics network ensures seamless transportation from anywhere in the world.

2. Storage & Separation

Proper storage and separation are crucial for safety and efficiency. Our secure, climate-controlled warehousing protects materials from environmental degradation and prevents potential hazards. Upon arrival, batteries are carefully sorted and classified based on type, chemistry, and condition to optimize the recycling process.

3. Recycling

We partner with leading recycling facilities equipped to recover over 90% of battery materials. These partners utilize advanced technologies, including hydrometallurgical and pyrometallurgical processes, to maximize material recovery. They prioritize safe and sustainable extraction, minimizing waste and environmental impact.

4. Refining

The battery recycling process yields "black mass," a valuable mix of cathode and anode materials including lithium, nickel, cobalt, graphite, copper, and aluminum. These recovered materials are then reintroduced into battery manufacturing, lessening the environmental footprint.
