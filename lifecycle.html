Resale & Redeployment

A large portion of recovered equipment is refurbished for resale and redeployment, thereby extending their useful life. After disposition is determined, all equipment slated for redeployment or resale is cleaned, tested, repackaged, and offered with an industry leading warranty. We have technology specialists with experience in selling used telecom and communications equipment through channels such as resellers, retailers, brokers, client staff purchase programs, and directly to network operators worldwide. When redeployment is scheduled, our processes streamline the refurbishment and equipment upgrade process. All of our programs are customized and open to future adjustments to increase efficiencies and asset valuations in all facets of the asset lifecycle. Agile, Smart & Convenient.

Valuations

We provide complete analysis of secondary market trends and valuation drivers. Our experienced team of specialists provides a low-to-high estimated resell value to help our clients make key business decisions. We offer the facility for client staff to purchase refurbished assets prior to any offering to our buyers. Our programs offer discounts, warranties, and have no liability for our clients. For assets not purchased by client staff, our extensive network of vetted buyers ensures competitive offers and efficient liquidation, maximizing returns beyond initial low-to-high estimates.
